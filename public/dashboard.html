Welcome, Professor!

Our app provides a secure and easy way to conduct online quizzes for your students.
  • 🔒 Protections & Features:
    • Single Submission Enforcement: Each student can only submit the quiz once. Attempts to re-submit are automatically blocked.
    • Google Authentication: Only authorized students (based on email list) can access your quiz.
    • Registration Number Verification: Students must enter a valid registration number to start the quiz.
    • Full-Screen Mode: Students are required to take the quiz in full-screen to minimize distractions and prevent switching tabs.
    • Auto-Save & Anti-Cheat: Quiz answers are auto-saved, and suspicious behaviors (like repeated reloads) are flagged.
    • Set a Timer: When creating a quiz, you can set a start and end date & time to automatically control when the quiz is available.
📝 To Create a Quiz, Please Prepare:
  • A CSV or Excel file of your quiz questions (MCQs, short answer, etc.)
  • A list of student email addresses who are allowed to take the quiz
  • Optionally, images for your questions (see below for upload instructions)
🖼️ How to Upload Images Using Postimages:
  1. Go to postimages.org
  2. Click "Choose images" and select your image, or drag and drop it.
  3. After upload, right-click the image and select "Copy image address".
  4. Paste the image link in your quiz question wherever you want the image to appear.
Your image link should look like: https://i.postimg.cc/abc123.jpg
