Welcome, Student!

Before you begin your quiz, please read these important guidelines carefully:

⛔ DON’Ts – What Not To Do During The Quiz:

  • Do not try to open other tabs, windows, or apps while taking the quiz. Full-screen mode is required and switching may auto-submit or disqualify your attempt.
  • Do not refresh, reload, or close your browser during the quiz. You may lose your answers or be locked out.
  • Do not use multiple devices or browsers to access the quiz. Only your first login will count.
  • Do not attempt to submit the quiz more than once. Only your first submission will be accepted.
  • Do not share quiz questions, answers, or screenshots with anyone. Academic honesty is expected.
  • Do not impersonate another student or use someone else’s Google account or registration number.

✅ Additional Guidelines:

  • Ensure your internet connection is stable before starting.
  • Have your registration number and Google account ready.
  • Read each question carefully and answer within the given time.

❓ Facing Problems?

If you face any issues with the quiz, immediately contact your professor.
Do not try to bypass or troubleshoot on your own, as this may affect your submission.
